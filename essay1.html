Thoughts on Photography

2025.10.05

The interplay of light and shadow is always the most enchanting theme in my lens. Every time I press the shutter, it's a brief embrace with time.


Photography is not just about capturing what we see, but about capturing how we feel. The beauty lies in the imperfection, the fleeting moments that never come again.


Those unplanned, unposed seconds are what make a photograph truly alive. I chase light, not perfection.
